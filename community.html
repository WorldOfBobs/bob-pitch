02 — Why This Matters
Free tools change the game for people who couldn't afford to play.
Most productivity software is priced for Silicon Valley salaries. We're building for everyone.
Raycast charges $96/year. Alfred charges $34. CleanShot is $30. Paste is $30. It adds up — and most people just go without.
Bob replaces all of them. For free. Forever. MIT licensed — the code belongs to the community.
This is the kind of tool that levels the playing field. A developer in Lagos gets the same setup as a developer in San Francisco.
We're not building for a niche. We're building for everyone with a Mac. That's 100 million people who don't have a tool like this yet.
03 — Your Role
No money. Just your time, skills, and voice.
The community that launches Bob Board with us becomes part of the story. Here's how you can contribute — pick what fits you.
🧪
Beta Tester
Download the app, break it, report what you find. Real feedback from real users is more valuable than any QA team. We need honest opinions — not hype.
📢
Ambassador
Post about Bob Board. Share it with your network. Post in Facebook groups, Twitter, LinkedIn, WhatsApp communities, Reddit. One genuine post from a real person beats any ad.
🎬
Content Creator
Make a short video showing one Bob tool in action. A 60-second demo of Color Bob or Clipboard Bob gets more downloads than a press release. You don't need a studio.
💬
Community Mod
Help manage the Discord. Welcome new members, answer basic questions, keep the vibe positive. The first 100 users set the culture — you help shape it.
🌍
Translator
Help translate the website and app descriptions into your language. Software that speaks someone's language gets installed. French, Yoruba, Igbo, Hausa — all valuable.
🛠️
Developer
If you code — even a little — there's something for you. Open issues on GitHub, suggest features, help fix bugs. This is open source. Your name goes in the commit history.
